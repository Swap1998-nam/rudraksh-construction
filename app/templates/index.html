The Company

Who We Are

Rudraksh Construction is a full-service building company delivering ground-up construction, renovation, and structural engineering across residential and commercial sectors.

We hold every project to one standard: measured, engineered, and finished exactly to spec. From the first site survey to the final walkthrough, our teams coordinate design, structure, and execution under one roof — so nothing gets lost in translation between the drawing and the build.

Registered and operating in Karnataka, we bring precision, safety, and accountability to every structure we touch.
